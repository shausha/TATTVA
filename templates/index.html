TATTVA: Transforming Ancient Texts into Virtual Archives

Prof. B. Santhi
SASTRA Deemed University

Opening Remarks
In a world grappling with challenges like mental well-being, health crises, governance complexities, and legal dilemmas, can the answers to some of our most pressing problems lie buried in ancient wisdom? The Sanskrit texts, a treasure trove of unparalleled knowledge, hold solutions to questions we’re still asking today. From holistic healthcare principles to advanced administrative strategies, they encompass it all. Yet, this treasure remains locked in an encoded form.
Introduction
The question before us is: How can we leverage AI and novel technologies to decode these timeless insights? And more importantly, how can we, as innovators and contributors, propel India to the pinnacle of success by unlocking this wisdom for the world? This is not just a challenge—it is our opportunity to bridge the past and the future.
Preserving and Sharing Knowledge
We bear a profound responsibility to reiterate and emphasize the value of our ancient knowledge for current and future generations. Our ancestors were not just scholars of their times but interdisciplinary experts, mastering diverse domains with remarkable proficiency. They exemplified mental and physical strength, enabling them to address everyday challenges with wisdom and resilience.
Ancient Texts and Interdisciplinary Brilliance
Our ancient texts are a testament to the interdisciplinary brilliance of our ancestors, encompassing all walks of life. For instance, Brihat Samhita provides invaluable insights into diverse fields, from agriculture to astronomy. The Sulbasutras delve into the nuances of mathematical concepts, laying the foundation for advanced thought. The Sushruta Samhita serves as a cornerstone of medicine and Ayurveda, offering techniques for a healthy and balanced life.
