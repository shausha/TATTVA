About TATTVA


TATTVA (Transforming Ancient Texts into Virtual Archives) is a pioneering project aimed at transforming India's ancient Sanskrit texts into a digital repository of knowledge. By leveraging AI and advanced OCR technology, the initiative focuses on:


  • Unlocking the vast wisdom embedded in ancient texts for current and future generations.
  • Making this invaluable knowledge accessible worldwide through digital formats.
  • Preserving and sharing the teachings of our ancestors for research, education, and global collaboration.
  • Bridging the gap between the rich cultural heritage of the past and the technological advancements of the future.
  • Creating a sustainable platform for the enrichment and advancement of humanity through timeless knowledge.
